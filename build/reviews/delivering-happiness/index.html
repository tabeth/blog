deliverying happiness

I have a huge amount of respect for Tony Hsieh. In his book, Delivering Happiness, he explained, very thoroughly how his company, Zappos, was able to have a very unique, friendly culture and still be a pretty profitable company, eventually being acquired by Amazon for over $1 billion. I’m very passionate about being independent, and as an extension of that, very passionate about being an entrepreneurial type, if not an entrepreneur itself. I’d recommend this book if only for the writing style.
